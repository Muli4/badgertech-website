Our Services

At Badgertech Solutions Ltd, we offer a wide range of professional safety and security services tailored to meet the unique needs of schools, malls, hospitals, commercial buildings, and homes.

From certified fire extinguisher supply and CCTV installation to clean agent suppression systems and PPE supply — our team delivers reliable, compliant, and scalable solutions across Kenya.

Portable Fire Extinguishers

We supply, install, refill, and maintain all types of portable fire extinguishers tailored to various environments including offices, schools, malls, kitchens, industries, and residential estates.

  • CO₂, Dry Powder, Foam, and Water-based extinguishers available
  • Monthly servicing & annual inspection
  • Installation as per OSHA & NFPA standards
  • On-site refilling and pressure testing
  • Custom signage and mounting accessories included

Electric Fencing

We design and install powerful electric fencing systems tailored for residential, commercial, and industrial security. Our fences are built to deter intruders and integrate seamlessly with alarm systems for instant alerts.

  • High-voltage fencing with alarm response
  • Integration with security lights and sirens
  • Remote monitoring and smart controls
  • Installed with quality insulators and energizers

CCTV & Access Control

Secure your premises with intelligent surveillance and access solutions. We provide cutting-edge systems to monitor, control, and protect entry points, enhancing security and accountability in your organization.

  • HD and IP CCTV camera installations
  • Night vision, motion detection, and mobile access
  • Biometric access control and RFID systems
  • Integration with alarms and intercoms
