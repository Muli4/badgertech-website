Our Services

We offer a wide variety of safety and security services designed to protect lives, property, and businesses across Kenya.

Portable Fire Extinguishers

We supply, install, refill, and maintain portable fire extinguisher units to ensure preparedness and compliance in all facility types.

Electric Fencing

We design and install reliable electric fence systems with alarm integrations for perimeter protection in residential and commercial properties.

CCTV & Access Control

We install modern CCTV systems, biometric access control, intercoms, and remote monitoring to enhance security and access management.

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

We supply a range of PPE including overalls, boots, gloves, helmets, face shields, and ear plugs for personal and industrial safety.

Fire Drill & Safety Training

We conduct fire marshal training, safety awareness sessions, and fire drills tailored to institutions, companies, schools, and hotels.

Fire Alarm & Suppression Systems

We design, install, and maintain fire detection systems and clean agent suppression systems for kitchens, server rooms, and offices.

Fire Pumps & Hydrant Systems

We supply and install high-performance fire pumps and complete hydrant systems for large buildings, malls, and industrial premises.

General Electrical Works

We handle wiring, fittings, solar backup, lighting and general electrical supply and maintenance for homes and commercial buildings.
