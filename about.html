About Us

Badgertech Solutions Ltd is a proudly Kenyan company that provides professional fire safety and electronic security systems. Since our founding in 2018, we’ve grown from a small team of freelance technicians to a nationally recognized safety solutions provider. In 2024, we officially incorporated, reinforcing our commitment to quality, integrity, and client satisfaction.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive fire and safety solutions that surpass expectations, ensuring the utmost protection for our clients and their assets through innovation, integrity, and technical excellence.

Our Vision

To become the foremost provider of cutting-edge fire and safety solutions in Kenya and the region — setting the standard for innovation, compliance, and customer satisfaction.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence – We go beyond expectations in everything we do.
  • Integrity – We act with honesty, transparency and professionalism.
  • Innovation – We embrace smart technology and forward-thinking solutions.
  • Customer-Centricity – We put the client’s needs first.
  • Continuous Improvement – We keep growing and getting better.

Our Story

Badgertech began as a group of safety-conscious professionals offering fire extinguisher installation and security systems to small businesses in Nairobi. Through word-of-mouth, hard work, and consistent service delivery, we quickly expanded our services and footprint nationwide.

Today, we install advanced fire suppression systems, electric fences, hydrants, and access control systems for schools, hotels, malls, and manufacturers across Kenya. Our physical office in Nairobi enables us to coordinate projects efficiently and serve clients faster.
