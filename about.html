About Badgertech Solutions Ltd

We are a proudly Kenyan company delivering fire safety and electronic security systems with precision, professionalism, and passion. Since 2018, we've grown into a nationally recognized safety partner for institutions and businesses.

Our Story

Badgertech Solutions Ltd began in 2018 as a small group of freelance safety technicians. Armed with passion and a commitment to make Kenyan homes, institutions, and businesses safer, we started by offering basic fire extinguisher services and security installations in Nairobi.

Through consistent service, professionalism, and referrals, we expanded our services to include fire suppression systems, electric fencing, CCTV surveillance, hydrant systems, and access control technologies. We served schools, malls, factories, and hotels—ensuring safety was no longer optional, but essential.

In 2024, we officially incorporated as Badgertech Solutions Ltd, reinforcing our commitment to certified service delivery, nationwide coverage, and technical excellence. Today, we are a trusted name in safety systems across Kenya.

Mission

To provide comprehensive fire and safety solutions that surpass expectations, ensuring the utmost protection for our clients and their assets. We are dedicated to delivering exceptional services tailored to meet the unique needs of each customer, backed by our unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and integrity.

Vision

To become the foremost provider of cutting-edge fire and safety solutions, setting the benchmark for excellence and innovation in the industry. We aspire to safeguard lives, protect property, and promote peace of mind for our clients across Kenya and beyond.

Sustainability & Safety Commitment

At Badgertech Solutions Ltd, sustainability and safety are embedded in every solution we provide. Whether it's fire suppression systems or electronic surveillance, we implement technology that enhances safety while reducing environmental impact.

We prioritize clean agent fire suppression systems, energy-efficient surveillance installations, and recyclable personal protective equipment. Our training programs promote safe handling of equipment and emergency response protocols to safeguard lives and infrastructure.

Our goal is to deliver reliable safety solutions that protect today and preserve tomorrow — responsibly and sustainably.

Innovation & Technology

Whether you need AI-powered surveillance, GSM-linked alarms, or integrated fire and access solutions — we deliver future-ready protection for every space.
