About Us

Badgertech Solutions Ltd is a proudly Kenyan company that provides professional fire safety and electronic security systems. Since our founding in 2018, we’ve grown from a small team of freelance technicians to a nationally recognized safety solutions provider. In 2024, we officially incorporated, reinforcing our commitment to quality, integrity, and client satisfaction.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive fire and safety solutions that surpass expectations, ensuring the utmost protection for our clients and their assets through innovation, integrity, and technical excellence.

Our Vision

To become the foremost provider of cutting-edge fire and safety solutions in Kenya and the region — setting the standard for innovation, compliance, and customer satisfaction.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence – We are committed to delivering the highest standards of quality in every service we provide, from installation to training. We believe in going above and beyond expectations to ensure safety, satisfaction, and reliability.
  • Integrity – At the heart of our business is trust. We maintain honesty, transparency, and professionalism in all our client interactions, ensuring that every engagement is guided by ethical conduct.
  • Innovation – As safety and security technologies evolve, so do we. We adopt modern tools like clean agent suppression systems, access control, and surveillance tech to keep our clients ahead of threats and compliance standards.
  • Customer-Centricity – We listen closely to our clients' needs and tailor our services accordingly. Whether it’s a school, mall, hospital, or private residence, we provide personalized, affordable, and scalable safety solutions.
  • Continuous Improvement – We constantly invest in staff training, service refinement, and updated tools to ensure we stay relevant, efficient, and competitive in the industry. Learning and improvement are embedded in our DNA.

Our Story

Badgertech Solutions Ltd began in 2018 as a small team of freelance technicians dedicated to improving fire safety awareness across Nairobi. Our early days were spent walking into shops, schools, and apartments offering to inspect extinguishers, install basic systems, and educate tenants on safety. Our commitment to honest work and our affordable pricing quickly earned us referrals, and our portfolio started growing organically.

Over the years, we expanded our services to include high-end fire suppression systems, electric fencing, CCTV surveillance, and access control technology. We invested in certification, compliance, and tools to serve clients at a professional level. By 2024, we became fully incorporated — Badgertech Solutions Ltd — signaling a new era of structured operations, corporate service delivery, and national reach.

Today, we serve a diverse range of clients across Kenya including learning institutions, commercial buildings, malls, manufacturers, and residential estates. With a growing team of trained experts and a physical office in Nairobi, we continue to build trust and deliver safety — one system at a time.
